About Jami

Hi there!

I'm Jami, and I'm so glad you've found your way here. Photography has always been more than just a profession for me; it's my passion and a way to connect with the beautiful stories that make us who we are.

I believe in the power of genuine moments and the magic that happens when people feel truly seen. Whether it's a joyful family gathering or a quiet, candid moment, my goal is to capture the essence of your story with authenticity and warmth.

I find immense joy in helping families feel at ease in front of the camera, allowing their true selves to shine through in every shot. With years of experience behind the lens, I've developed a knack for capturing those fleeting, heartfelt moments that might otherwise go unnoticed.

I take pride in creating a relaxed and enjoyable environment where you can be yourself, laugh freely, and share your most cherished connections. My approach is all about celebrating the uniqueness of each family, pulling out the laughter, love, and joy that make your story special. I can't wait to meet you and help preserve the moments that matter most!

If this resonates with you, and you'd like to book a session, let's schedule a quick call to get to know each other.
