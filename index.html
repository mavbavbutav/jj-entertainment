About

Meet John & Jami Ferguson.

We’re a husband-and-wife team in the Nashville area who’ve spent years turning our creative passions into small businesses. Jami runs a wedding and portrait photography studio and a luxury flower wall rental company. John captures cinematic aerial footage and builds modern digital tools for other entrepreneurs.

JJ Entertainment Solutions is how we keep it all in one place — a single home for the people, couples, and small businesses who want to work with us.
